AI meets Security

I'm Kavindu Heshan, an ICT undergraduate at the faculty of Technology, General Sir John Kotelawala Defence University. My work sits at the intersection of artificial intelligence and cybersecurity — building systems that are not only intelligent but hardened against modern threats.

From training ML models for predictive analytics to engineering secure IoT automation systems, I'm constantly bridging the gap between academic learning and real-world application. My goal is to become a leading AI Security Engineer, defending digital infrastructure with intelligent, ethical, and robust solutions.

  • Passionate about ethical AI and data governance
